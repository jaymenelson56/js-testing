This crash course will introduce you to the fundamental concepts of software testing and give you a little experience implementing unit tests yourself. It is not an exhaustive course, but getting your feet wet, starting to build good habits, and demonstrating a budding interest in quality assurance will all serve you well as you embark on your career as a web developer or software engineer.
